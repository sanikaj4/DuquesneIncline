GET INVOLVED

Be part of preserving one of Pittsburgh's most iconic landmarks. Join our community and help keep the Duquesne Incline running for generations to come.

The Society for the Preservation of the Duquesne Heights Incline

The Society for the Preservation of the Duquesne Heights Incline was formed in 1964 by the Duquesne Heights/Mount Washington neighborhood group that had rescued, repaired, and reopened the Incline in 1963. The purpose was to continue to restore and rehabilitate the cars, equipment, and both stations, and to establish the entire operation as a reliable commuter facility and a popular visitor attraction.

Our dedicated members help ensure this historic Pittsburgh landmark continues to serve the community and delight visitors from around the world. Member benefits include:

  • Annual complimentary rides
  • Exclusive invitations to special events
  • Quarterly newsletters with behind-the-scenes updates
  • Discounts at the Duquesne Incline gift shop
  • The satisfaction of helping preserve a historic treasure

Memberships in the society are available. Call for details or fill out an application form.
