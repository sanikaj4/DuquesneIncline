Hours and Fares

Open 365 Days a Year

Hours: 6:30 a.m. – 12:30 a.m.

No reservations or advance tickets required!

FARES

Standard Rates
  • Adults (Ages 12-64): $2.50 Each Way | $5.00 Round Trip
  • Children (Ages 6-11): $1.25 Each Way | $2.50 Round Trip
  • Children 5 & Under: Free
  • University of Pittsburgh, Carnegie Mellon, & Chatham University Students: Free with valid ID
Group Rates (10+ people)
  • Adults (Ages 12-64): $1.75 Each Way | $3.50 Round Trip
  • Children (Ages 6-11): $1.00 Each Way | $1.75 Round Trip

PAYMENT OPTIONS

  • Cash fares only (Exact fare preferred)
  • Port Authority Bus Passes & Tickets Accepted

SENIOR DISCOUNT

Seniors (65+) ride free with a BLUE or YELLOW PRT card or a Senior ConnectCard.

WHEELCHAIR ACCESSIBILITY

  • Available at the Upper Station (1220 Grandview Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA 15211).
  • Incline cars can accommodate wheelchairs up to 25 inches in width.
